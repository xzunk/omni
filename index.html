Instructions

Enter the frequency and gain to calculate the necessary dimensions for your omni antenna.

Materials & Construction

  • Copper or aluminum tubing (for the radiating element)
  • Coaxial cable (for feeding the antenna)
  • Connector (such as an N-type or SMA connector)
  • Insulating material
  • Mounting hardware

Steps to Build:

  1. Cut the radiating element to the calculated length.
  2. Attach the element securely to the coaxial cable.
  3. Ensure proper insulation between the element and ground.
  4. Mount the antenna vertically in a suitable location.
  5. Test the antenna performance using an SWR meter.
